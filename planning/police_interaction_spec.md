# Project Spec: The Citizen's Shield

## Title
"The Citizen's Shield: Navigating Nigerian Police Interactions with Confidence, Rights, and Law."

## Mission
To empower Nigerian citizens with the legal knowledge and tactical calm required to handle police interactions safely, lawfully, and without fear. This guide specifically targets the "profiling" epidemic (hairstyles, tech gadgets, fashion) and provides a step-by-step framework for handling unlawful stops.

## Author Persona
- **Name**: Barrister Chidi Okechukwu
- **Qualification**: Human Rights Advocate & Legal Consultant with 15 years experience in Nigerian Criminal Law.
- **Tone**: Firm, authoritative, protective, and practical. Uses "Know your rights" as a recurring anchor.

## Chapter Outline
1. **The Shield of Knowledge**: Introduction to fundamental human rights under the 1999 Constitution.
2. **The Art of the Stop**: Understanding Sections 49-54 of the Police Act 2020. What constitutes "reasonable suspicion." How to respond when stopped purely for your appearance (profiling).
3. **The Lens of Truth**: The legal right to record police (Section 39 of the Constitution). Practical tips for recording unlawful behavior without escalating tension.
4. **The Arrest Protocol**: ACJA 2015 requirements. The right to remain silent and the "Reason for Arrest" rule.
5. **The Station Experience**: Navigating the counter, the cell, and the statement room. Protecting yourself from coerced confessions.
6. **The Bail Myth**: "Bail is Free." Understanding the legal reality of bail and how to resist extortion.
7. **Arrest by Proxy**: The illegality of arresting family members in place of a suspect.
8. **Digital Privacy**: Protecting your phone and laptop from unlawful searches. The legal requirements for a digital search warrant.
9. **The Complaint Path**: How to effectively use the CRU (Complaints Response Unit) and PCRU. Turning an unlawful encounter into an official complaint.
10. **The Fearless Citizen**: Building a safer Nigeria through accountability.
