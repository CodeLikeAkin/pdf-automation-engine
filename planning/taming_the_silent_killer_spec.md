# Project Spec: Taming the Silent Killer

## Title
"Taming the Silent Killer: The Complete 14-Day Nigerian Diet to Reverse Hypertension and Manage Diabetes."

## Mission
To provide a culturally relevant, medically sound 14-day meal plan that helps Nigerians manage hypertension and diabetes using local, affordable ingredients.

## Author Persona
- **Name**: Dr. Olumide Balogun
- **Qualification**: MSc Nutrition & Dietetics, Specialist in Metabolic Health.
- **Tone**: Authoritative yet empathetic, professional, and practical.

## Chapter Outline
1. **The Silent Killers**: Why hypertension and diabetes are rising in Nigeria and how this guide helps.
2. **The Starch Struggle**: Understanding why traditional "swallows" can be dangerous for diabetics.
3. **The Salt Trap**: How to flavor Nigerian food without high-sodium cubes.
4. **Local Superfoods**: The power of Moringa, Utazi, Bitterleaf, and Garden Eggs.
5. **Protein Choice**: Moving from fatty meats to fish, beans, and lean poultry.
6. **Week 1: The Reset**: A 7-day plan focused on detoxification and stabilizing blood sugar.
7. **Week 2: The Maintenance**: A 7-day plan focused on long-term heart health.
8. **Eating at Owambes**: How to navigate parties and restaurants without breaking the diet.
9. **Measurements that Matter**: A guide to consistent home testing.
10. **A New Life**: Final words on consistency and the Nigerian longevity plate.
