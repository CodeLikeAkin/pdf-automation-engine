# The Art of Mindful Living
design_system: A

## Introduction
Mindfulness is the basic human ability to be fully present, aware of where we are and what we’re doing, and not overly reactive or overwhelmed by what’s going on around us.

## Why Design Matters
A well-designed document helps the reader focus on the content. By using **System A (Warm Editorial)**, we create a sense of tranquility and wellness.

### Key Principles
- Presence
- Awareness
- Acceptance
