# Skill: The Humanizer
**Objective**: Remove common AI linguistic patterns and add human texture, voice, and rhythm to content.

## Reasoning Rules
1. **Identify AI Patterns**: Look for "excessive politeness," "hedging," "perfectly balanced lists of three," and robotic transition words (e.g., "In conclusion," "Moreover," "Firstly").
2. **Break the Rhythm**: AI writes in consistent sentence lengths. Humanize by mixing very short sentences with long, flowing ones.
3. **Inject Specificity**: Replace generic AI examples with "hyper-local" or specific, gritty details.
4. **Active Voice**: Force the use of active verbs. Remove passive "is being" or "has been" structures.
5. **Wikipedia Check**: Cross-reference against the "Signs of AI Writing" list and systematically eliminate them.

## When to load
Load this skill at the *Final Edit* stage of any draft.
