# Skill: Doc Co-Authoring
**Objective**: Build complex documents (books, specs, guides) through iterative, deep-thinking collaboration.

## Methodology
### Phase 1: Context Gathering
- Ask the user for the "Why," "Who," and "What."
- Identify the target reader's current knowledge gap.
- Define the "Single Big Idea" of the document.

### Phase 2: Refinement & Build
- Work section-by-section. Never skip to the end.
- Use "Surgical Editing": Suggest changes to specific paragraphs rather than rewriting the whole file.
- Maintain a running "Decision Log" of why certain content was included or excluded.

### Phase 3: Fresh Reader Test
- Simulate a reader who is skeptic or confused.
- Identify "blind spots" where the author assumed too much context.
- Ensure the transition between chapters is seamless.
