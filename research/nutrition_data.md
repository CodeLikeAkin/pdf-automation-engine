# Research Summary: Nigerian Diabetic & Hypertension Nutrition

## High-Priority Staples (Low Glycemic Index)
- **Acha (Fonio)**: Extremely low GI, heart-healthy grain.
- **Unripe Plantain**: High in resistant starch; best for stabilizing blood sugar.
- **Whole Wheat / Millet**: Better alternatives to refined flour swallows.
- **Ofada Rice**: Unpolished, high-fiber rice.

## Forbidden/Limited Staples (High Glycemic Index)
- **Pounded Yam**: High GI; causes rapid sugar spikes.
- **White Garri/Eba**: High GI; should be consumed very sparingly with high-fiber vegetables.
- **White Bread / Refined Flour**: Should be eliminated in the Reset phase.

## Natural Seasoning (Sodium Alternatives)
- **Iru (Locust Beans)**: Local fermented beans, rich in protein and flavor.
- **Dawadawa / Ogiri**: High-umami natural seasonings.
- **Garlic, Ginger, Turmeric**: Natural anti-inflammatories.
- **Scent Leaf / Curry Leaf**: Strong aromatic profiles to compensate for less salt.

## Key Nutrients for High Blood Pressure
- **Potassium**: Abundant in Ugu, Garden Eggs, and Pawpaw.
- **Magnesium**: Found in beans and leafy greens like Ewedu.
- **Fiber**: Essential for slowing glucose absorption.

## 14-Day Structure Logic
- **Week 1 (The Reset)**: Focus on low-carb, high-fiber, and plant-based proteins to stabilize insulin.
- **Week 2 (Maintenance)**: Introduce moderated complex carbs and lean proteins for long-term sustainability.
