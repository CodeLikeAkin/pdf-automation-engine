# Research Context: Fact-Checking & Data Retrieval

In this room, we provide the "Truth Layer" for the books. Our goal is to ensure every claim is backed by reliable data or legal frameworks.

## Research Standards
- **Accuracy**: Double-check Nigerian laws (ACJA, Constitution) and health guidelines.
- **Tone**: Professional, non-sensationalized, and high-authority.
- **Context**: Focus on the Nigerian market (e.g., local food names, specific police divisions).

## The Fact-Checking Loop
1. **Identify Claims**: Scan drafts for technical or legal claims.
2. **Verify**: Use search tools or internal knowledge items.
3. **Inject Data**: Update the `research/` folder with verified data points for the Drafter to use.

## Supporting Automation
- Provide `query` suggestions for the Unsplash cover fetcher.
- Verify that metadata claims (e.g., "Complete 14-Day Diet") are reflected in the content.
